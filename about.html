Company's History

ALPHAMATIC ENERGY SERVICES limited is a privately-owned Nigerian company based in Port Harcourt,
with a focus on providing engineering, procurement, construction (EPC), and maintenance services.
It operates primarily within the oil and gas industry but also serves other sectors such as power,
petrochemicals, and water management.


Founded in Port Harcourt, the company has expanded its capabilities to deliver high-quality solutions
for a wide range of industrial needs, from tank cleaning to platform maintenance. With a commitment
to quality and continuous innovation, ALPHAMATIC ENERGY SERVICES provides end-to-end solutions from
design and engineering to construction and commissioning.

Our Vision

To provide comprehensive engineering and supply chain solutions that consistently and adequately exceed our clients' expectations for quality, service, and value.

Our Mission

To be recognized as the partner of choice within the industries we serve, known for our unwavering commitment to quality, integrity, and sustainable growth.
